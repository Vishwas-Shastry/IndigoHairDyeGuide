How to Use 3V Indigo Dry Leaves for Hair Coloring

Step 1: Prepare the Leaves

Start by grinding the indigo dry leaves into a fine powder using a mortar and pestle or a grinder.

Step 2: Mix with Water

Mix the powdered indigo with warm water to create a smooth paste. The consistency should be like yogurt.

Step 3: Application

Apply the paste evenly onto your hair, starting from the roots to the tips. Use gloves for better application.

Step 4: Wait and Rinse

Leave the paste on your hair for about 1-2 hours. After that, rinse thoroughly with water.

Step 5: Final Touches

Condition your hair after rinsing. Enjoy your beautifully colored black hair!
